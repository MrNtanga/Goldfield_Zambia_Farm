Application for Student

To apply for admission at the Goldfield Zambia Farm Institute, follow these steps:

  1. Download the application form from our website.
  2. Fill in all the required details accurately.
  3. Attach the necessary documents, including academic certificates, ID, and passport-sized photos.
  4. Submit the completed application form along with the attachments to our admissions office.
  5. Wait for an acknowledgment of receipt and further instructions via email.
